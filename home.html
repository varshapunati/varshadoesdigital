about me

Hello there, I’m Varsha Punati. I would say I am and always have been a creative. I find beauty in the mundane, and my creative avenues stretch from graphic design to Indian classical dance to photography.


I find joy in capturing the moments in between. Movement is happening all around us at all times, and I try to bottle as many subtleties as I can with my lens.


I have been behind the camera since I was a child, taking pictures and learning from my mom. Now I will admit I wasn't a fan of sitting behind the camera as a kid, but it helped me develop my keen eye. A skill that I carry with me in all art forms I participate in.
